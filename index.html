The Dark Knight

Batarang is bat-shaped throwing weapon used by Batman. The name is derived from Bat and Boomerang. Original spelling of the object was Baterang. Although named Batarang, they act more like shurikens than Boomerangs. One of Batman's useful tools in his arsenal. Most probably the most used tool in his arsenal. The Batarang is often used as a range weapon for long distance combat. Batman usually uses the Batarang in order to knock out guns used by the enemies.

Batman's true identiy is Bruce Wayne. He is a multi-billionaire and owner of Wayne Enterprise. Bruce Wayne is a wealthy American playboy, philanthropist. He inherited his wealth after the murder of his parents. Both of his parents were killed before his eyes. He witnessed the murder of his parents, firsthand. His father was Dr. Thomas Wayne, and his mother was Martha Wayne. After the murder of his parents, he swore vengance against criminals, and bring them to the law.

Batman fights crime in Gotham City. He is often assisted by his butler, Alfred. Alfred raised him after the murder of his parents. Batman prefers to work alone, although he does have friends fighting along his side. Friends such as Robin, Nightwing, Batgirl and others. He has many enemies that try various ways in killing him but none have succeeded. Enemies such as The Joker, Penguin, Two Face, Scarecrow, Bane, and many others. Catwoman is switches roles between and ally and foe to Batman.

Batman has no superpowers but still fights crime. He relies on his intellect, physical prowess, martial arts, detective skills, science, technology, wealth as well as intimidation to fight against his foes. Batman will wound his enemies but he will never kill anyone. He says "If you kill a killer, there's still the same number of killers." It is said Batman with enough prepartion time can beat anyone and everyone.
